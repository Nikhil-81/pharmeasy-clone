Frequently Asked Questions

What are the sneh_benifits of PharmEasy Plus?

Plus members get extra 5% cashback on medicine/healthcare orders with free delivery and extra 10% cashback on diagnostics orders along with free doctor teleconsultations. Also, you won't be charged any convenience fees for pay on delivery.

How long is my PharmEasy Plus membership valid for?

Depending on the plan you choose, your PharmEasy Plus membership is valid for 3 months or 6 months from the date of purchase of the program.

When will I get the cashback?

Cashback will be credited into your PharmEasy wallet within 48 hours of order delivery.

What is the validity of the cashback?

Cashback earned through the PharmEasy Plus Membership will not have any expiry.

Is there a cap on the maximum amount that can be earned through the 5% cashback on Medicine/Healthcare orders?

Yes, based on the value of your order, a 5% cashback up to ₹200 will be credited for Medicine/Healthcare orders.

Is there a cap on the maximum amount that can be earned through the 10% cashback on Diagnostic Tests?

Yes, based on the value of your order, a 10% cashback up to ₹200 will be credited for Diagnostic Test orders.

Is free delivery applicable for all orders?

For orders placed during your membership period, free delivery is applicable on orders above ₹199 for 50 orders when opting for standard delivery. For priority delivery the additional delivery fee will be applicable. However, if your order weighs more than 1 Kgs, standard delivery charges based on weight will be applicable.

Can I cancel my PharmEasy Plus Membership?

No. Once enrolled, you will not be able to cancel your PharmEasy Plus membership.

Can I renew my PharmEasy Plus membership?

Yes, once your existing membership has expired, you will see an option to buy the membership again if you are eligible. However, PharmEasy Plus membership prices are subject to change.

Can I use other offers/coupons along with the PharmEasy Plus Cashback?

Yes, you can use any other offers/coupons along with the PharmEasy Plus membership.

How do I avail PharmEasy Plus cashback?

Cashback will be auto-applied on all Medicine, Healthcare & Diagnostics orders. No need to apply any coupon code.

Are there any restrictions on PharmEasy Wallet usage?

No, there are no restrictions on the usage of your PharmEasy wallet. You can use it in full against any of your orders.

Is there a limit on the number of orders I can get PharmEasy Plus Cashback on?

Yes, this benefit is available on a maximum of 50 medicine/healthcare products orders and 50 lab tests booked during your membership.

How many Doctor Consultations can I avail through PharmEasy Plus Membership?

As a Plus member, you can avail 20 free doctor consultations during your membership period. Feel free to get in touch with our doctors whenever you are in need. We strongly advise that you take any medication only after consulting with a registered medical practitioner.

What’s the process for consulting the doctor?

You can submit a request for doctor consultation after getting enrolled from your membership details page.

Can I get a faster delivery through PharmEasy Plus?

While we would love to provide faster delivery, this option is not available under the PharmEasy Plus membership sneh_right now.

Can I buy PharmEasy Plus via COD?

Yes, you can buy the PharmEasy Plus membership via COD only if you buy it along with your medicines or healthcare products order. For COD order, first add your medicine or healthcare items to cart and then select the Plus membership.

Can I avail the cashback on Diagnostics tests in all cities?

We are expanding rapidly. For now, you can avail this cashback only if our diagnostic services are available in your city.

Are there any products where plus sneh_benifits are not eligible?

Yes, cashback sneh_benifits are not applicable on baby food products that cater to infants under the age of 1.
